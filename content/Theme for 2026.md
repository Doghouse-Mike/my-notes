## Clarifying 2026

### The Year of Out.

- Get out more
  \- Bikes
  \- Gigs
  \- "Experiences"
  \- Seeing friends/family
- Clear out
  \- The house
  \- My brain
  \- Lists
  \- Get a laugh "out" of Shiv, every day

So those are the two sub-themes I reckon.

Just added "What have I done today that helps get _out_?" to my daily note template. Accidentally that's pretty good. It could pertain to actually doing something directly aligned to the theme of Out, but also to things that clear the way for doing that. Agreeing to/arranging something for a future "out" would count as a contribution.

I also liked chatty g's idea that "If it required shoes, it counts."

"- "One deliberate Out session per week" (bigger, planned)" sounds good too. During the colder months, that could even count as taking the dogs for a walk.

### "Out" Menu

**Important**- any one of these counts. The menu can change as time goes on.

OUT (Experiences)

- Go for a walk
- Ride my bike
- See one person IRL
- Attend something slightly inconvenient
- Maintain correspondence/comms

OUT (Objects)

- Remove 5 items from one drawer/other place
- Identify one "why do I still own this?"
- Make a donation bag
  - Get rid of a donation bag
- Photograph items to sell (even if you don't list them yet)

> Every item I remove is one less decision Future Me has to make.

## Monthly Reflection Questions

Once a month, answer these briefly (bullet points only):

1. Where did I feel most "out" this month?
2. What made going out easier than expected?
3. What resisted being thrown out — and why?
4. Did anything I removed create unexpected relief?
5. Is "out" currently meaning movement or lightness more?

## Mid-decision Questions:

- "Does (doing) this move me out or keep me in?"
- "Will this come with me?"
- "Shoes or sofa?"

_**"If in doubt, out."**_
