## The "Wisdom"

- If you're leaving somewhere with a functioning toilet, use it before you go.
- Always accept the first tea/coffee/other beverage someone offers you, even if you don't particularly want it right now. If you say "no" to the first one, there might not be a second offer. 
- Don't whole chest "that could never happen" while being presented with evidence that it has happened.
