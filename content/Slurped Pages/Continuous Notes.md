# Continuous Notes

## [🧠 Second Brain](https://brain.ssp.sh/)

Search

Icon to open search

Last updated Mar 11, 2025

This is the essence of a [Second Brain](https://www.ssp.sh/brain/second-brain) and the [Future of Blogging](https://www.ssp.sh/brain/future-of-blogging) of blogging. Instead of old blogs that hold a wealth of information but need to be updated over time, continuous notes are **updated frequently** and win over time instead of losing value.

> Note-taking is like saving money with interest. You save not only the interests of each year but also interest from interests. The same happens if you start taking notes; ideas will [compounding](https://www.ssp.sh/brain/compounding-note-taking). Your notes will not be worth their years. But multiplied.

## # Comparing Notes vs. Blogs

](https://www.ssp.sh/brain/continuous-notes/#comparing-notes-vs-blogs)

I like the differentiation between notes and Personal Blogs. If I have a minor issue, something that I learned today (TIL), or anything else, I can add it to my second brain as a small note that I can update continuously. Whereas a blog usually dives deeper, compiling multiple notes into one, un-updated.
