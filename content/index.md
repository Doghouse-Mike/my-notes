## Listening

### [Nine Inch Noize](https://music.apple.com/gb/album/nine-inch-noize/1893171321)

Some thoughts:

- Some of these songs I have been listening to for 20+ years
- What if NIN, but from the future? (maybe instead of the 80's)
- Literal goosebumps on more than one occasion 
- Play it loud, and/or on good cans

Much like all the old punk bands singing about how work/highschool sucks and falling in love with teenage girls, there's something a *little creepy* about a 60 year old man and his songs of being angry/horny. Tunes are good though. Some solid gold bops.
