## Session Round Up (From Splat)

The battle continued in the Sith temple, with the motley crew of alliance operatives and reactivated KX-series security droid dispatching wave after wave of enthralled Shorlocks.

Some of the Shorlocks even appeared to be able to will loose debris to attack the group, but they were still soundly defeated.

In the aftermath, the group found their objective, a black cuboid artifact, was located on the central daís. The artifact produced a heavy cloud over the area and appeared to be leeching not just surrounding light, but life itself, harming whoever touches it (as Rimer was unfortunate enough to find out).

If the Emperor were to get his hands on his prize, who knows what devastation could have been wrought with its use.

McGuffin in hand, the new team fled the temple through the way they entered and, via deception and stealth, returned to Whisper Base, objective complete.

After reporting in to alliance command, warning was received of an impending riposte from the Imperials that established Whisper base.

There was just enough time to grab some *brief* rest, requisition some urgent supplies, get the Sith artifact off base, and prepare to defend the alliance's latest prize.

Will the supplies arrive in time?

Will the beast riders and Darrastead village heed the call?
