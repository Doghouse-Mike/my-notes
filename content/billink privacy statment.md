1. When and why does Billink process personal data?

Billink processes personal data exclusively for justified and transparent purposes

Which are related to its role as a payment solution provider. We determine the

Purposes and means of processing.

We process personal data in the following situations:

A. When using Billink as a payment method at a webshop When you choose at a webshop

For payment via Billink, we receive your personal data from the webshop to process your request

To assess and facilitate payment. We act independently as

Controller, separate from the webshop.

B. When requesting additional information from external parties In order to ensure the reliability of our services, we enrich the data received with information from

Third parties. For example:

- Address and identity verification
- Creditworthiness information
- Information from public registers such as the trusteeship or insolvency register

C. When contacting our customer service or visiting our website When you contact us or visit our website, we process the data you provide (such as name, email address, IP address and communication content) to process your request or the use of

Analyse our site

1. What personal data does Billink process?

We process the following data, among others:

- Identification data: name, gender, date of birth
- Contact details: address, email address, telephone number
- Payment details: purchase amount, status, bank account number
- Technical data: IP address, browser and device data
- Transaction information: order date, webshop, delivered goods or services
- Data from external sources: creditworthiness, insolvency status
- Communication details: content of emails or chat messages with Billink

We do not process any special categories of personal data, unless you explicitly and

Voluntarily provided to us, for example in the context of a payment arrangement.

1. With whom does Billink share your data?

Billink only shares personal data with third parties when this is necessary for

The execution of our services, based on our own

Responsibility for processing.

Categories of recipients are:

- Data verification and credit information partners
- Debt collection agencies and bailiffs in the event of default
- External registries (such as Rechtspraak.nl)
- Hosting and IT service providers
- Legal or tax advisors
- Parties to whom claims are transferred (such as Billink Finance B.V.)

In all cases where we engage processors, we enter into processor agreements

In accordance with Article 28 GDPR.

1. How long does Billink keep your data?

We do not store personal data longer than is necessary for the purpose for which they are

Collected, unless we are legally obliged to keep them longer.

Guidelines:

- Payment details: 7 years (tax retention obligation)
- Customer communication: 2 years after realling
- Collection files: up to 5 years after closure
- Marketing data: until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent

1. How does Billink secure your data?

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data

Protect. Among other things:

- Encryption via SSL/TLS
- Secure access to systems
- Strict access rights
- Regular security audits
- Storage in data centres with physical access control

Access to personal data is limited to employees for whom it is necessary.

1. What rights do you have?

You have the following rights under the GDPR:

- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to disposal
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to human intervention in computerised decisions

You can submit a request via privacy@billink.nl. For verification, we may ask you for a

Provide proof of identity. This will be removed after review.

You also have the right to file a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority via

Www.authority of personal data.com.

1. Changes

Billink may customise this Privacy Statement in the event of developments in legislation, case law or our

Service. The most up-to-date version can always be found on our website. In the event of major changes

We will inform you about this through appropriate channels.
