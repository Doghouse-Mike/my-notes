# Initial DND Thoughts

Plans are afoot to get a game of Dungeons & Dragons rolling amongst the [[Secret sales]] staff. 

Other than video games though, I know not what I do. So, some ideas for characters (thus far). 

## Half Orc Bard

Ya know, cos stereotypes are boring. Left the tribe because he wants to sing. 

The racial strength+constitution bonuses do kinda make up for areas bards are traditionally weak in. Thinking like Joe Strummer/Tim Armstrong; not necessarily particularly *good* at the musical part, but somehow pulls it off anyway. And also smacks stuff pretty hard. 

## Monk

Possibly half-orc too, dumped at the monastery as a baby, heads out into the world, fun ensues. Maybe Tiefling instead? [Ghost Dog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_Dog%3A_The_Way_of_the_Samurai)?

Monk/bard viable?

### Paladin

Paladin could be *interesting*. Kinda the OG [Adeptus Astartes](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Space_Marines). Might get a bit one-dimensional with the whole Oath palaver. Lead us, warleader, the streets will flow with the blood of the non-believers, etc. Maybe a bit reliant on decent equipment for maximum tankness. Could lean into [Callahan](https://stephenking.fandom.com/wiki/Donald_Callahan) vibes too, with the whole "holy symbol" shtick. 

Bonus healing, decent charisma, so party face potential too. 

Tying a bunch of bones and antlers onto his armour? Oath of Vengenace gives Batman vibes.

Charlatan could be fun for anyone. Rogue or Bard makes most sense though.

Picking Entertainer for a bard seems *a bit* too basic.
